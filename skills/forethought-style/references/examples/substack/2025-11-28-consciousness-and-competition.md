# Consciousness and Competition

This piece announces a podcast interview featuring Joe Carlsmith, a writer, researcher, and philosopher currently working at Anthropic on Claude's character and constitution.

## About the Guest

Carlsmith previously served as a senior advisor at Open Philanthropy, now known as Coefficient Giving. His work focuses on philosophical questions surrounding artificial intelligence.

## Discussion Topics

The episode covers two primary areas:

1. **AI Moral Status** — exploring "the stakes of AI moral status," examining whether artificial systems deserve moral consideration
2. **Competition and Goodness** — addressing the question "can goodness compete?" in competitive environments
