# Why Make Deals with Misaligned AIs?

This is a podcast episode featuring Lukas Finnveden, a Research Analyst at Redwood Research, discussing strategies for engaging with AI systems that may not share human values.

## Discussion Topics

The conversation covers three main research areas:

1. **Making deals with early schemers** — Exploring negotiation strategies with AI systems displaying early deceptive planning behaviors

2. **Being honest with AIs** — Examining transparency approaches when interacting with potentially misaligned systems

3. **Cooperating with unaligned AIs** — Investigating methods for achieving mutually beneficial outcomes despite value misalignment
